My Favourite Personality

Narendra Modi

The Honourable Prime Minister of India Shri Narendra Damodardas Modi is born on 17 September 1950 and has been the PM of India since 2014. He was the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014 and is the member of parliament for Varanasi. He was a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). He was born and raised in Vadnagar in northeastern Gujarat, where he completed his secondary education. In 2001,he was appointed Chief Minister of Gujarat and elected to the legislative assembly. He has done great work in 10 years making Indians proud and has done much work towards country.
